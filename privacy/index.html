1. WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT

        

          We have two types of users: domain administrators and end users. The
          types of information that we collect differs for the different users,
          however every user's full name and email address are provided when
          they log into Google and are stored. Every other piece of stored data
          is something explicity entered by the user.
        

        
    
          
  • 
            Domain Administrators: In order to function, we
            must store a calendar ID that can be referenced by end users.
            Actions taken by domain administators that impact other users are
            stored and a log is recorded for posterity.
          
    • 
          
  • 
            End Users: In order to use this service, the end
            user must grant access to their Google Calendar through the relavent
            Google APIs. No information is inherently stored, however there is
            an option to save data to our database for the user's convenience.
          
    • 
        

        
Error logs for all users are stored for troubleshooting.


        
2. WHO HAS ACCESS TO COLLECTED INFORMATION?


        

          No information collected by this service will ever be intentionally
          shared with a 3rd party except where required by law. No data will
          ever be sold. No data will ever be used to train AI.
        


        
3. HOW To ACCESS TO YOUR OWN DATA?


        

          Users interested in receiving a copy of their own data can request it
          by sending an email to contact@schoolcycle.app.
